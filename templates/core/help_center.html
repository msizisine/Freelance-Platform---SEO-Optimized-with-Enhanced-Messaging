Frequently Asked Questions

Creating an account is easy! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner, choose whether you're a homeowner or service provider, and fill in your details. You'll need to provide your email address, create a password, and verify your email address to complete registration.

Browse our service categories or use the search function to find professionals in your area. You can view profiles, read reviews, compare quotes, and directly message professionals to discuss your project. Once you find the right match, you can hire them directly through our platform.

We use a secure payment system where funds are held in escrow until the work is completed to your satisfaction. You can pay via credit card, debit card, or other supported payment methods. The professional gets paid after you approve the completed work.

Yes, we verify all professionals on our platform. They go through a screening process that includes identity verification, professional license checks (where applicable), and review of their work history. Look for the "Verified" badge on professional profiles.

If you're not satisfied with the work, first communicate directly with the professional to resolve any issues. If that doesn't work, you can request mediation through our support team. We have a dispute resolution process to help ensure fair outcomes for both parties.
