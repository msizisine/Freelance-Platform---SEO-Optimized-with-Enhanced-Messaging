Stay Safe on pro4me

Your safety is our top priority. Follow these guidelines to ensure a secure and positive experience for everyone.

For Homeowners

  • Always verify professional credentials before hiring
  • Check reviews and ratings from previous clients
  • Meet in public places for initial consultations
  • Never pay the full amount upfront
  • Keep all communication on the pro4me platform
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels off, it probably is

For Professionals

  • Complete your profile with accurate information
  • Verify your credentials and licenses
  • Be clear about your services and pricing
  • Meet clients in public spaces first
  • Document everything with photos and written agreements
  • Never start work without a confirmed agreement

During the Project

  • Communicate regularly and clearly
  • Take before and after photos
  • Keep receipts for all materials
  • Document any changes or issues immediately
  • Maintain professional boundaries
  • Follow local regulations and safety standards

Payment Safety

  • Use pro4me's secure payment system
  • Avoid cash payments when possible
  • Never share banking details directly
  • Understand the refund policy
  • Keep all payment receipts
  • Report any suspicious payment requests

Home Safety

  • Secure valuables before work begins
  • Ensure proper insurance coverage
  • Check permits for major renovations
  • Keep children and pets away from work areas
  • Verify compliance with building codes
  • Take photos of the work area before and after

Online Safety

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Never share personal financial information
  • Be wary of urgent payment requests
  • Report suspicious messages immediately
  • Keep your contact information private until needed

How to Report Issues

1

Document Everything

Take screenshots, save messages, and gather all relevant information about the issue.

2

Report to pro4me

Use our reporting system or contact support immediately with detailed information.

3

Cooperate with Investigation

Provide any additional information requested and follow up on your report.

4

Seek Legal Help if Needed

For serious issues, consider consulting with legal professionals.

Red Flags to Watch For

  • Requests for payment outside the platform
  • Pressure to make quick decisions
  • Vague or inconsistent information
  • Refusal to provide references
  • Unusually low prices that seem too good to be true
  • Requests for sensitive personal information

Best Practices

  • Read reviews from multiple sources
  • Get multiple quotes for comparison
  • Ask for proof of insurance and licenses
  • Have a written agreement for all work
  • Keep records of all communications
  • Trust verified professionals with good ratings
