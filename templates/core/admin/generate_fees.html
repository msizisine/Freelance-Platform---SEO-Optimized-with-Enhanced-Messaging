Monthly Fee Information
What are Monthly Service Fees?

Monthly service fees are charged to all active service providers to maintain their presence on the platform and access to features.

Fee Structure:
  • Standard Provider: R5.00/month
  • High Volume Provider: R15.00/month (10+ jobs)
  • Enterprise Provider: Custom pricing
When are fees charged?

Fees are typically generated at the beginning of each month and become due within 30 days. Providers can pay fees through their available earnings or external payment methods.

Fee Payment:

Monthly fees are automatically deducted from provider earnings when available. If insufficient earnings, providers will need to pay via other methods.

Tip: Review the provider activity before generating fees to ensure accurate calculations.
